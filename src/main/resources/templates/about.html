Get to know us :)

Unwanted or disappointing gift? Maybe you just don't want it anymore? We're ReGit and we're here for you. Don't let that cute stuff go to waste! Maybe someone else can make use of that?

The rules are simple:

1. We don't sell things. You got that for free, so let it stay that way. You can pick another gift for yourself in exchange. That's how we work - gifts are gifts.

2. If you're interested in some gift just contact the post owner - contact info (email address) is attached to the post. Then you can discuss any details.

3. If you want to give away your gift just post the gift with some description. You can add other contact information if you want. We're only publishing your email.

4. If you made a deal with another user, please remove your post. It will help everyone ;)
